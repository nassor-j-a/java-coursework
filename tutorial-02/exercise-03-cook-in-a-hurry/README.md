# Exercise 2-3 – Boiling Time Calculation

## Problem
Compute time required to boil water in a cylindrical pot.

---

## Given

- 2 minutes per litre
- 1 litre = 0.001 m³
- Volume of cylinder = π × r² × h

---

## Formula

```text
radius = diameter / 2

volume = π × r² × h

litres = volume × 1000

time = litres × 2
